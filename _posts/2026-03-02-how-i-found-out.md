It's impossible not to spot something as big as an A380. It was almost floating. But it shouldn't be here in Prague at this time. 

I pulled up the avgeek's best friend. Flight EK212 from Houston to Dubai had been diverted here. Then came another A380. This time from Athens.

It clicked. My heart sank.
