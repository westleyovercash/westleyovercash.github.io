date: 2026-07-14

We meet for coffee every week. Her eyes widen with surprise each time.

She orders for us. When the mood strikes, I splurge on a cappuccino. I take the same seat. I know it's the same seat because of the hot chocolate stain. The barista gifts us one branded napkin.

An hour and a half passes—sometimes two. Then I apologize for taking too much of her time.
