The windows in the Airbnb on the third floor flung open for 72 hours. The three Germans with their boombox set up camp for the weekend. 

Each jumped to take a turn sitting in the window, puffing curls of smoke. One looked like a younger version of DJ Scooter. "Da ist" echoed up and down the street.   

They stumbled out this morning at 10.30. The blonde one waved off the open bottle of wine.
