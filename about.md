We yearn for words that make us feel.

Not just the words, but the breathing room in between them.

That's why I write.

On a tram in Prague, a man bragged to his friend about writing the best report of his career. Yet he couldn't recall a single line of it—an LLM had written it.

"If you can't beat 'em, join 'em," he said.

I love the craft too much to join them. If you're reading this, we already have something in common.

I'm only a quick email or call away. We can start talking about the story your audience will retell.
