She opened a bottle of red that cost more than my rent. I sipped myself into another mission.  

Linda peered through her gray curls and toasted me. Two of the crispiest-crust pizzas waited for us at Anchovies—under a different name. I only needed to make it back to her flat with them unnoticed.

Inside Anchovies, I asked the barman, "Do you have an order for Stanley?"
